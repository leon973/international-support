Department of International Student Services

Our Goals

  • To provide international students with a unique multicultural environment to enhance their intercultural and social skills, and to prepare them for careers in the globalized word of the 21st century.
  • To provide support services to all international students from enrolment until the completion of their studies.
  • To assist in all non-academic matters of international students ranging from visa, welfare and etc.
